Best Post Workout Snacks

 

If you are trying to lose weight you might be inclined to skip the post workout meal to save calories. However this is not but a mistake. You should focus on post workout meal to maximize the impact of your training session and to recover faster for the next workout. Wholesome post workout meal will also prevent excessive hunger that can cause overeating later in the day.

  1. Peanut Butter Banana Smoothie
  2. Cottage Cheese With Orange
  3. Boiled Eggs With Light Mayo
  4. Low Fat Chocolate Milk
  5. Baked Sweet Potato With Egg
  6. Quinoa Salad
  7. Turkey Sandwich
  8. Spinach & Chicken Salad
  9. Mushroom Spinach Omelette
  10. Pan Fried Salmon
